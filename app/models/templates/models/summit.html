Kipoi Summit

New horizons in Computational Regulatory Genomics

Join us for an exciting two-day conference September 25 and 26 at the top of Zugspitze, the highest mountain in Germany, as we bring together a small group of scientists that are interested in regulatory genomics.

About

The Kipoi Summit is a two-day conference dedicated to computational regulatory genomics featuring four keynote presentations, a panel discussion, oral presentations based on abstract submissions, and a poster session. This goal of the event is to bring together experts and researchers to share cutting-edge research, exchange knowledge, discuss current challenges and foster collaboration.

  • Dates: September 25-26
  • Cost: Free!
  • Abstract submission deadline: July 7
